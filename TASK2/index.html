Questions & Answers

1. What does fr stand for?

fr stands for fractional unit. It is used to divide available space into equal parts.

2. How many columns are created?

Three equal columns are created.

3. What happens when screen size is reduced?

The columns adjust their width according to the screen size.

4. Do items overflow or adjust?

Items adjust automatically to fit inside the grid container.

5. Why is fr better than px for layouts?

fr is flexible and responsive, while px is fixed and does not adjust with screen size.
