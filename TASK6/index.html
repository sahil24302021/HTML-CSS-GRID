Questions & Answers

1. What are grid lines?

Grid lines are the lines that divide rows and columns in a grid layout.

2. How many columns does the item span?

The item spans two columns.

3. What happens to other items?

Other items move to the next available space in the grid.

4. Can two items overlap?

No, grid items do not overlap unless forced using positioning.

5. Where is column spanning used in websites?

Column spanning is used for headers, banners, and highlighted sections.
